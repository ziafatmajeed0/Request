Delete Your Account

If you want to delete your account and associated data from SahulatKaar, you can request deletion at any time. Below are the steps, what will be deleted, and what may be retained for legal reasons.

How to request account deletion

Contact us

  • Email: Help.SahulatKaar@gmail.com
  • Phone: 03426520389
  • Include your registered email or phone number so we can identify your account.

We’ll verify your identity for your security before proceeding.

What data will be deleted

Data removed on deletion

  • Your profile (name, email/phone)
  • Your uploaded images (profile or other)
  • App usage history (requests, tasks, etc.)
What data may be retained

Legal & compliance hold

  • Some data (e.g., financial transactions, invoices) may be retained up to 90 days if required by law.

After this period, the data will be permanently deleted.

Processing time

When will this be done?

  • Your request will be processed within 30 days.
